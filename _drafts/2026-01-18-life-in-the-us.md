###### Are you sure you want to do a Master's in Computer Science in the US at a top university?

It's been 5 months since I first landed in the US. I remember scanning the airport looking for "extraordinary" things like my parents discussed when they first landed. I'm finally here! I was excited and scared. I didn't want people to think I'm a new non-immigrant but I was tired after a 16 hour long flight with 0 hours of sleep (don't ask why). I was scared because I didn't want them to deny me entry (😭), I have worked super hard.
